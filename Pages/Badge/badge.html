Badge


            @import url("/Pages/Badge/badge.css");
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="/Pages/Badge/badge.css">

Badge on Icons:

5
9

To use the badge component, use the class 'badge' inside a div and insert an img tag to use the specific badge icon. You can also use another div with class 'badge-count' to show a number.


            <div class="badge">
                <img src="/Assets/cart.svg" alt="">
                <div class="badge-count">5
            
            <div class="badge">
                <img src="/Assets/mail.svg" alt="">
                <div class="badge-count">9

Badge on Avatars:

There are 3 types of badge on avatar. Use a div with class 'badge-status online','badge-status doNotDisturb', or 'badge-status offline' to show the current status of the user.


            <div class="badge">
                <img class="avatar" src="/Assets/AvatarImg1.jpg" alt="">
                <div class="badge-status online">
            
            <div class="badge">
                <img class="avatar" src="/Assets/AvatarImg1.jpg" alt="">
                <div class="badge-status doNotDisturb">
            
            <div class="badge">
                <img class="avatar" src="/Assets/AvatarImg1.jpg" alt="">
                <div class="badge-status offline">
